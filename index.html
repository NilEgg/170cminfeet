Understanding Height Conversion: 170 cm in Feet

Converting between centimeters and feet is a common need when comparing height measurements across different systems. 170 cm equals 5.58 feet, or 5 feet and 7 inches when expressed in the standard feet and inches format. This conversion is particularly useful when navigating between the metric system used in most countries and the imperial system used primarily in the United States.

Why Convert 170 cm to Feet?

Understanding your height in both centimeters and feet allows for easier communication across different measurement systems. A height of 170 cm is considered average in many countries that use the metric system. When converted to the imperial system, 170 cm in feet equals approximately 5.58 feet (5 feet and 7 inches), which provides context for those more familiar with feet and inches.

Average Heights Around the World

The average height for men in the United States is 175.4 centimeters, which is about 5 feet 9 inches. The average height for women is 161.7 centimeters, which is about 5 feet and 3 1/2 inches. This is according to a 2018 report published by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).1

A height of 170 cm (5 feet 7 inches) is slightly below the average for American men but above the average for American women. In many European countries, the average male height ranges from 175 cm to 180 cm, while the average female height ranges from 162 cm to 170 cm. Therefore, 170 cm represents a height that falls within common global averages.

How to Convert 170 cm to Feet

The conversion from centimeters to feet follows a simple mathematical formula: divide the length value by 30.48, since 1 foot equals 30.48 centimeters.

Formula: Feet = Centimeters ÷ 30.48

For 170 cm: 170 ÷ 30.48 = 5.577 feet ≈ 5.58 feet

To convert the decimal part of feet to inches, multiply it by 12 (since 1 foot = 12 inches):

Formula: Inches = (Decimal Part of Feet) × 12

For 0.58 feet: 0.58 × 12 = 6.96 inches ≈ 7 inches

Therefore, 170 cm equals 5 feet and 7 inches.
